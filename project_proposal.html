Youssef's WebTech Project Proposal

What is the objective of your website?

The objective of the project is to build a website for the company (startup) I interned with last summer of 2024. As a Business Intelligence intern, my plan was to start working on the company's website, since it will contribute a great deal to its reputation. The website will include mutliple pages that show not only information about the company, but also the industry it operates in. The company is a VC-backed asset manager that invests in renewable energy, specifically solar and wind. Website visitors will be able to see a homepage with the overview about the company and the services they provide. They will also be able to navigate through pages such as: Team, Industry News, Contact Us, and more.

Who is the target audience for your website?

The target audience for my website is visitors who are, for the most part, either investors or renewable energy developers. These visitors are usually likely want to know more about the company, or they may already know it and are looking to partner with it.
