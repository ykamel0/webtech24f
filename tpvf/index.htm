Active Equity Group was founded in the wake of the passage of the Inflation Reduction Act of 2022 to provide expertise in an industry undergoing a sea change. Having worked in other tax credit industries, consulted for some of the primary investors in the space, and worked at several syndicators of various sizes, we saw the opportunity to create a different approach.

Navigating the Energy Transition

The US renewables industry is shifting like never before. Macro trends, world-leading subsidies, and soaring energy demand from the AI boom have pushed an already increasing sector into new territory, making it the most attractive renewable industry globally. With experience in over $4bn of renewable energy investments, our team has the expertise to simplify the process.
